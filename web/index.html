Average Grade
The grades are in the german academic grading system, where 1.0 is the best possible grade and 5.0 is the worst. The average grade is a weighted average of all my grades up until now. Almost all grades are weighted with their number of ECTS credits. Three courses from the first semester are weighted with half their ECTS credits.

Average Percentile

Average Z-Score
The lower, the better (a negative number is good). The Z-Score is a standardized score that allow to data points from different distributions. The standard score is calculated by subtracting the mean and dividing by the standard deviation. The units are standard deviations. The average shown here is made from the Z-scores calculated separately for each course and is weighted by ECTS and number of students in that course. We can see that in average my grades are about one standard deviation better than the mean grade in that course.
